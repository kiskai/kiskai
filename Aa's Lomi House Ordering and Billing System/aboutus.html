ABOUT US

Aa's Lomi House is located at Barangay Tatlong Maria in Taal Batangas near the Shrine of Our Lady Of Caysasay, Mr and Mrs. Kim and Sharmaine, the owners of the resto established the lomi house on June 19 2020. The restaurant offers a variety of delicious lomi and different filipino style pancit. With the addicting taste of Aa's Lomi there is no doubt that people really loves the batangas lomi that is why they keep coming back. Aside from it's "sik-sik" of toppings, Aa's Lomi House offers you a special lomi which are the must try in our menu list the "Volcanic Lomi" and the "Lechon Lomi" not only that but we also offers a variety of filipino style pancits such as: Pansit chanmi, guisado, bihon, mike bihon, canton and canton bihon. If you are still hungry you might also try our special add ons, which are: Chicarong Bulaklak, Siomai and more extra toppings on your Lomi. We assure you that all of the aftermentioned is "swak" and student friendly meal so what are you waiting for? Tara na sa Aa's Lomi House!!
